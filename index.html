Welcome ! The lab is headed by Professor Kevin Whittingstall, Ph.D and focuses on the detection of even the most subtle changes in neurovascular coupling. For this, we are developing methods that can simultaneously track neural activity (EEG) and blood flow (fMRI) in the brain with great spatial and temporal precision.
